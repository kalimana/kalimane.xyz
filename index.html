55+ Pro Stealing Strategies

Master the art of stealing with these advanced techniques and strategies used by pro players.

Basic Stealing Techniques

1. Adjacent Base Strategy

Always steal from bases adjacent to yours. The closer the target, the higher your success rate and lower chance of getting caught.

2. Timing is Everything

Wait for the perfect moment when the base owner is distracted or away. Rush jobs usually fail.

3. Inventory Management

Always ensure your inventory has enough space before attempting a steal. Clear out low-value items first.

4. Team Coordination

Ask friends to help by distracting the target player with 1v1 battles while you execute the steal.

5. Server Selection

Keep rejoining servers until you spawn next to wealthy players or near the spawner for optimal positioning.

Advanced Pro Techniques

1. The 30-Second Trap Method

Use invisibility cloak to sneak behind bases, wait for the perfect timing, then execute a quick steal and teleport back.

2. Robux Base Opening

Pay 39 Robux to open locked bases when the owner just locked down and won't return for 60+ seconds.

3. Event Timing Exploitation

Execute steals during major events when players are distracted by rare spawns or admin activities.

4. Multi-Account Banking

Use a secondary account as a bank to store valuables, making you immune to counter-theft.

5. Trait Machine Patience

Wait and observe brainrots passing through trait machines before claiming - patience can multiply your earnings 5x.

Essential Equipment for Stealing

Money Calculation Formula

Final Income = Base Income × (Mutation Multiplier + Trait Multiplier)

Base: $35/s × Gold (1.25x) = $43.75/s

Defense Tactics

1. Layer Your Defense

Use multiple traps + sentry + personal monitoring for maximum security.

2. Stay Alert

Watch for theft notifications and return to base immediately.

3. Base Design

Keep valuable brainrots in the back, place defenses strategically.

4. Counter-Attack

Use Rage Table, Taser Gun, or Medusa Head to stop active thieves.
